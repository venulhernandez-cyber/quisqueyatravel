🇩🇴 Why the Dominican Republic?

The Caribbean, Done Right

More beach, more culture, better value — here's why millions of travelers choose the DR every year.

✈️

Easy to Reach

Direct flights from New York, Miami, Toronto, London, Madrid and Paris. 3–4 hours from the US East Coast.

💰

Best Value in the Caribbean

World-class all-inclusive resorts at prices significantly lower than Jamaica, Barbados or St. Lucia.

🏖️

Incredible Beaches

Punta Cana's Bávaro Beach consistently ranks among the best in the world. Warm, calm, crystal-clear water year-round.

🍽️

Amazing Food & Culture

Sancocho, mofongo, fresh seafood on the beach — and a culture built around music, family and celebration.
